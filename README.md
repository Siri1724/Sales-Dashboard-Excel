# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across multiple years, countries, products, and customer segments. The dashboard provides executives and business users with key insights into revenue, profit, profitability, discounts, and sales trends through an intuitive and visually appealing interface.

## 🎯 Project Overview

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights by allowing users to explore performance from different perspectives using interactive filters and dynamic visualizations.

---

## ✨ Features

- Interactive slicers for:
  - Year
  - Country
  - Customer Segment
- KPI Cards
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Profit
  - Profit Margin
  - Units Sold
  - Discounts Given
  - Top Product
- Annual Sales vs Profit Comparison
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Profit by Product Ranking
- Profit by Customer Segment
- Sales & Profit by Country
- Clean and executive-friendly dashboard design

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting
- Formulas
- Dashboard Design Principles

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Compare yearly sales and profit performance.
- Identify the highest-performing products.
- Analyze profitability by customer segment.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Evaluate sales and profit across different countries.
- Track overall business KPIs at a glance.
